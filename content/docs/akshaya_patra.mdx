In today’s fast-paced world, most people are busy with their work schedules, appointments, and other obligations so cooking their food and concentrating on their health has become a deep concern. Most people have been ordering food through food serving apps like Zomato and Swiggy and encountering multiple health issues ranging from viral fever to typhoid due to unhygienic conditions and expired ingredients used in the food by restaurants to make quick profits.

What if there is an automated and AI-assisted food machine that can prepare any type of cuisine ranging from Italian pasta to South Indian dosas? Our intelligent and AI-enabled Akshaya Pathra is a cooking companion that works on recreating granny’s or mom’s kitchen (multiple recipes) in your kitchen.

## Features of Akshaya Pathra
The significant features have been described as follows:

### Supports Endless Recipes and Food Types
Our innovative product Akshaya Patra allows you to prepare any type of food and recipes ranging from Mexican Pizza, Japanese Sushi, South Indian thali etc. The machine is designed to ensure that all ingredients are mixed thoroughly before the cooking process to serve delicious meals to multiple people at once. The machine is equipped with a storage device that can store ‘n’ number of recipes and enables you to prepare your food by reloading your recipe into the machine, enabling you to serve your food faster than any human chef.

This machine provides you with a hassle-free experience by enabling you to simply load your ingredients, set the existing recipe and start cooking. Within 10 minutes, the machine delivers your favourite food with restaurant quality at your fingertips.

### Easy Installation, Modular, and Portable
Akshaya Patra is designed in a way that it is suitable for any type of kitchen ranging from hotels, and restaurants to home kitchens. Our modular and portable machine allows you to transit from one location to another hassle-free with minimal transport capabilities. This machine supports user-friendly installation, which means anyone can set up and install it with minimal supervision by following the instructions from the manual that we provide along with the product.

Our machine not only cooks food but also supports quick cleaning and easy maintenance. Each component can be removed easily and cleaned using normal water, enabling hygiene and maintaining the quality of food each time you cook.

### Embedded AI Intelligence
Our product Akshaya Pathra is equipped with AI intelligence at its core, enabling you to load your recipes and customise them to make your personalised meal. The algorithms in our machine are programmed to learn from the existing recipes, your cooking habits, and the ingredients you load each time, allowing you to choose your dietary needs based on your lifestyle. It can even recommend adjustments in the recipes and cooking style based on your lifestyle goals.

This machine is equipped with an elegant and unique console on the top to set accurate temperature, and time for cooking to serve a delicious meal for your taste buds. This machine can be controlled using an app installed on your mobile device with an internet connection from anywhere.

### Inbuilt Multi-language and Voice Assistance
Akshaya Pathra has been designed to meet the needs of a customer throughout the world whether the customer is from Japan or Mexican. People across the globe speak multiple languages ranging from English, Chinese, Japanese and many more. Our product is programmed to support multi-languages not only on the console but also over built-in voice commands.

Akshaya Pathra supports in-built voice assistants like Alexa and Google Assistant to give commands over voice to control its operations. When you are busy with other house chores, you just have to say “Alexa, start preparing Biryani” or “Hey Google, prepare my dinner” after loading ingredients into the machine. With voice assist control, you can command your instructions without physical work, you just must use your voice.

### Your Kitchen Companion
With many AI features built in, Akshaya Pathra is not just a food-cooking machine, but it is more than your granny’s or mom’s kitchen with pre-loaded recipes. Whether you prepare a simple meal or a family dinner, Akshaya Patra ensures that every dish is prepared with precision and delicious taste and texture.
